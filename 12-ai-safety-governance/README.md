# AI Safety & Governance Module

The threat model, defenses, and regulatory landscape for production LLM systems.

## 🚀 Roadmap

### 1. Threat Model for LLM Applications
- Attack surface (prompt, data, tools, output)
- STRIDE applied to LLM apps
- Risk assessment matrix

### 2. Prompt Injection & Defenses
- Direct vs indirect injection
- Delimiters, spotlighting, structured prompts
- Detection classifiers

### 3. Jailbreak Attacks & Red-Teaming
- Known jailbreak families
- Automated red-team tools (Garak, PyRIT)
- Internal red-team programs

### 4. Output Filtering & Classifiers
- Llama Guard, NeMo Guardrails
- Classifier chaining
- Blocking vs rewriting

### 5. PII Handling
- Detection, redaction, minimization
- Data retention policies
- Prereq: `07-data-engineering-for-ai/05_pii_detection`

### 6. Bias, Fairness, Model Cards
- Bias evaluation methods
- Fairness tradeoffs
- Model cards and datasheets

### 7. Copyright, Licensing, Training Data
- Training data disclosure
- Output copyrightability
- Fair use considerations

### 8. Regulatory Landscape
- EU AI Act risk tiers
- NIST AI RMF
- SOC2 implications for AI features

### 9. Responsible AI Policy & Governance
- AI review boards
- Policy frameworks
- Vendor risk assessment

### 10. Incident Playbooks for AI Harms
- Detection and triage
- Disclosure and remediation
- Post-mortem templates
