# Industry Use Cases & ROI Module

How AI creates business value across domains, and how to measure it. Covers patterns, anti-patterns, and ROI frameworks AI Architects need when talking to leadership.

## 🚀 Roadmap

### 1. AI Value Framework
- Automation vs augmentation vs creation
- Where LLMs beat classical ML
- Where LLMs lose to classical ML

### 2. Customer Support & Service
- Deflection, assist, triage patterns
- Metrics: CSAT, AHT, resolution rate
- Common failure modes

### 3. Sales & Marketing
- Lead scoring, outbound, content gen
- Personalization at scale
- Attribution challenges

### 4. Software Engineering
- Code gen, review, debugging
- Developer productivity metrics
- Prereq: `15-ai-developer-experience/`

### 5. Legal, Compliance, Finance
- Document review, contract analysis
- Risk screening
- Regulatory constraints

### 6. Healthcare & Life Sciences
- Clinical documentation, ambient scribe
- Claims and prior-auth
- HIPAA and safety boundaries

### 7. Knowledge Work & Internal Ops
- Meeting summarization
- Enterprise search (bridges to `09-rag/`)
- Policy Q&A

### 8. ROI Calculation Frameworks
- Cost model (tokens + infra + ops)
- Value model (hours saved, quality lift, revenue)
- Formula: `ROI = (benefits − costs) / costs`

### 9. Build vs Buy vs Fine-Tune
- Decision matrix
- TCO comparison
- Vendor risk

### 10. Business Case & Stakeholder Communication
- Problem → metric → architecture → ROI narrative
- Executive summary templates
- Common pushbacks and responses

### 11. Pilot to Production Playbook
- Scoping a pilot
- Graduation criteria
- Scaling pitfalls

### 12. Risk & Governance from a Business Lens
- Reputational risk
- Regulatory risk
